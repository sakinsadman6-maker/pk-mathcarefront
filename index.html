Powerful features for

From question creation to result publication — the complete ecosystem for student success.

📝

Smart MCQ Builder

Teachers can quickly add questions with multiple options, mark correct answers, and organize by subject or topic.

⏱️

Scheduled Exams

Schedule exams with a start date, time limit, and automatic locking. Students can only access during the exam window.

🏆

Live Leaderboard

Auto-published results with 1st, 2nd, 3rd place podium and full ranking. Celebrate every student's achievement.

🔍

Mistake Review

After each exam, students can review every question — see what they got wrong and the correct answer explained.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Track performance over time. See score trends, weak topics, and improvement areas with visual charts.

🔐

Secure Login

Separate login for students and teachers. Students register, teachers have admin panel access with a special code.

How it works

Teacher creates.
Students compete.
Results appear.

1

Teacher adds MCQ questions

Use the admin panel to create questions, add 4 options, mark the correct answer, and assign to a subject.

2

Schedule the exam

Set the exam title, duration, date/time window. Students can access only when the exam is live.

3

Students take the exam

Students log in, see available exams, and complete MCQs within the time limit.

4

Auto results & leaderboard

Results publish instantly. Leaderboard shows 🥇🥈🥉. Students review mistakes and learn.
